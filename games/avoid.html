☄️ Meteor Rain

Move your mouse / drag your finger to dodge the meteors. Score = seconds survived.

Meteor Rain drops you into a constant meteor shower that keeps speeding up. It's a free hard avoid game online with no safe pattern to memorize — just fast reflexes and a bit of luck to survive one more second.
