🔢 Number Rush

0.0s

Tap 1 to begin — find every number in order

Tap 1, 2, 3… all the way to 25, in order, as fast as you can. Every wrong tap adds a +1s penalty. Lowest time wins.

Number Rush is a free online speed and focus game built on the classic Schulte grid: find and tap every number from 1 to 25 in order, as fast as possible, while a single wrong tap costs you a time penalty. Simple to explain, brutally hard to do quickly.
