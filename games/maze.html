🌀 Wire Maze

Enter the START box, then trace the dark corridor to GOAL before the timer runs out. Touch the bright area = back to start. Mobile: drag your finger.

Wire Maze is a free steady-hand maze game online: trace your cursor through a narrow corridor to the goal before the clock runs out, and touching a wall sends you straight back to start. Simple to understand, genuinely hard to finish.
