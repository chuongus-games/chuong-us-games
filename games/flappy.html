🐤 Flappy Neon

Tap / click / press Space to fly. Pass a gap to score 1 point.

Flappy Neon is a hard flappy-style tap game you can play free online, right in your browser. The rule is simple — tap to fly through the gap — but scoring past 10 makes this one of the hardest flappy-style games around.
