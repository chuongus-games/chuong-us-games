🏃 Spike Runner

Tap / Space to jump over the spikes. Speed increases forever — score is distance.

Spike Runner is a free hard endless runner game online. Jump the spikes, survive the speed increases, and see how far you can get — a simple format that turns into one of the hardest runner games once the pace picks up.
