🧱 Neon Breaker

Move your mouse / drag your finger to steer the paddle. Tap / Space to launch. Clear every brick — the board refills faster each time. Miss once and it's over.

Neon Breaker is a free hard brick-breaking game online: keep the ball alive with your paddle while clearing full boards of bricks. Every cleared board brings back a fresh set at a slightly faster ball speed, so staying alive for a long run is what makes this one of the hardest brick games you'll find in a browser.
