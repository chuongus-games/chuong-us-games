🔵 Hardest Dodge

Use arrow keys / WASD (or drag with your finger) to move the blue square from START to GOAL. Touch a pink bullet = back to start.

Hardest Dodge is a free online bullet-dodging game built for players who want a real challenge. Guide the blue square through spinning bullets to the goal — one touch and it's back to start, making this one of the hardest dodge games online.
