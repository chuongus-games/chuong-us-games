💥 Neon Siege

Move your mouse / drag your finger to weave through incoming fire from every side. Score = seconds survived.

Neon Siege drops you in the middle of an open arena under fire from every direction at once. It's a free hard survival game online with nothing to memorize — bullets are aimed at you the instant they spawn, so only continuous movement and quick reflexes keep you alive one more second.
